# Automated Software Specifications

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_1
- Description: The system shall provide a catalog of free meditation and relaxation content, including guided meditations and soothing sounds, that can be accessed without a subscription.
- Source Persona: Serenity Seeker
- Traceability: Derived from review group relaxation enthusiasts
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user is not logged in, When they navigate to the meditation content section, Then they can access a selection of free meditation and relaxation content.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_2
- Description: The system shall allow users to track their meditation progress, including duration and frequency of sessions.
- Source Persona: Serenity Seeker
- Traceability: Derived from review group relaxation enthusiasts
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user has logged in, When they complete a meditation session, Then their progress is recorded and displayed in a progress tracker.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_3
- Description: The system shall provide an easy and transparent cancellation process for users who wish to cancel their subscription.
- Source Persona: Serenity Seeker
- Traceability: Derived from review group relaxation enthusiasts
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user is logged in and has an active subscription, When they navigate to their account settings, Then they can easily cancel their subscription and receive confirmation of the cancellation.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_1

- Source Persona: Serene Sleeper

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_2
- Description: The system shall offer a customizable sleep timer that allows users to set a specific duration for relaxation sessions or sleep stories, ensuring they are not disturbed during their designated sleep period.
- Source Persona: Serene Sleeper
- Traceability: Derived from review group health_conscious
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user has started a relaxation session or sleep story, When they set a sleep timer, Then the session or story shall automatically pause or end after the designated duration.

- Source Persona: Serene Sleeper
- Traceability: Derived from review group health_conscious
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user has used the app for a period of time, When they access their sleep report, Then they can view detailed statistics on their sleep quality, including duration, frequency of awakenings, and the relaxation techniques that were most effective.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_1
- Description: The system shall provide a curated list of free or low-cost health and wellness resources, clearly indicating which resources are free and which require payment.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Health Enthusiast
- Traceability: Derived from review group health_app_users
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user is searching for health and wellness resources, When they access the system, Then they can filter resources by cost, and see a clear indication of free versus paid resources.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_2
- Description: The system shall allow users to access useful health content without requiring payment or subscription, while also providing transparent information about content limitations for free users.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Health Enthusiast
- Traceability: Derived from review group health_app_users
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user is browsing health content, When they encounter a paywall or subscription request, Then they can access alternative free content, and see information on what content is available for free versus with a subscription.

# Requirement ID: FR_auto_3
- Description: The system shall verify and regularly update the availability and cost of listed health and wellness resources to ensure accuracy and minimize encounters with unexpected paywalls or subscription fees.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Health Enthusiast
- Traceability: Derived from review group health_app_users
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a user relies on the system for free or low-cost health resources, When they access a resource, Then they are not unexpectedly redirected to a paywall or subscription page, and can report inaccuracies to the system.
